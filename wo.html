Simple

4 day cycle

Day 1 - Push & Pull

  1. One arm chin ups superset with pseudo planche push-ups
    4 sets, 10 reps
  2. Wide grip pull-ups superset with dips
    4 sets, 10 reps
  3. Neutral pull-ups superset with diamond push-ups
    4 sets, 10 reps
  4. Lying back extensions superset with lalanne pushups
    4 sets, 10 reps

Day 2 - Legs

  1. Front squats superset with squat sits
    4 sets, 10 reps
  2. Lunges superset with lunge holds
    4 sets, 10 reps
  3. Leg press superset with lying leg curls
    4 sets, 10 reps
  4. Leg extension superset with seated leg curls
    4 sets, 10 reps
  5. Calf raises
    4 sets, 15 reps

Day 3 - Core

  1. Leg lifts
    4 sets, 10 reps
  2. Dragon flag
    4 sets, 10 reps
  3. Plank
    4 sets, 10 reps
  4. Side pank
    4 sets, 10 reps
  5. Lying back extensions
    4 sets, 15 reps
